Bangladesh

Bangladesh, my motherland is one of the most populous country in the worrld. She is most densly populated country in the world except some city states. Bangladesh possess the largest sea beach in the world, Cox's Bazar Sea Beach. The country achieved its independence in the year 1971.
Bangladeshi people has a history of movement for the sake of recognition for mother tongue. It's known as 'Bhasha Andolon' among Bnagladeshi people. The movement reaches its peak in 1952. On 21 February 1952 some students were shot dead by cop while they were in a procession demanding Bengali language to be recognized as state language.
